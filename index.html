WorldDownloader Version 4.1.1.0 for Minecraft 1.16.4 .

A minecraft mod that downloads a copy of a world on a multiplayer server for personal singleplayer use. This is a fork of @dslake's original version, updated to Newer Versions.

You can also try @uyjulian's liteloader version (which is based off of this one).

World downloader is maintained by Pokechu22.
